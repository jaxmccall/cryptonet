Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +
You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.
Why is my transaction pending? +
Transactions may be pending due to network congestion or additional verification requirements. Most transactions complete within 30 minutes to 2 hours.
How do I complete KYC verification? +
Go to your profile settings and select "Complete Verification". You'll need to provide a government-issued ID and a selfie for identity confirmation.
What are the withdrawal limits? +
For unverified accounts: $1,000 daily. Basic verification: $10,000 daily. Full verification: $50,000 daily. Institutional accounts have higher limits.
